About

The Best Education in Gaza Strip.

Khan Yunis Training College seeks to achieve its pioneering goal among the technical and applied academic institutions in the Gaza Strip and the region by making its graduates the most competitive in the local and international labour market by continuing to offer distinguished diploma programs in professional and technical specializations, and striving to establish bachelor's programs in applied sciences to keep pace with the changes. Environmental, economic and industrial development in the local and global labour market.
